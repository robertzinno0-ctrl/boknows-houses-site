How It Works

From first contact to cash in hand — here's exactly what to expect when you sell to Bo Knows Houses.

The 3-Step Process — In Detail

No surprises. No hidden steps. Just a simple, transparent process from start to finish.

1

Tell Us About Your House

Fill out our quick online form or give us a call at 1-941-271-7856. It takes less than 2 minutes. We just need the basics:

  • Property address and city
  • Rough condition of the home (don't worry — any condition is fine)
  • Your name, phone, and email so we can reach you
  • Optionally, your situation so we can tailor our approach

There's no obligation and your information is completely private. We don't share it with anyone.

2

Get Your Fair Cash Offer

Once we receive your info, our local Florida team gets to work. We'll research comparable sales in your area, factor in the condition, and come up with a fair cash number — usually within 24 hours.

We'll call you to walk through the offer, answer every question you have, and there's absolutely no pressure. You can take time to think it over.

  • 100% cash offer — no financing contingencies
  • No commissions or hidden fees
  • We cover all closing costs
  • The offer is yours to accept, reject, or counter
3

Pick Your Closing Date & Get Paid

If you accept our offer, you're in control of the timeline. Want to close in 7 days? Done. Need 60 days to find your next place? No problem. We work around your schedule.

We use a reputable local title company to handle all the paperwork. On closing day, you'll receive your cash — by wire transfer or certified funds. That's it. You're done.

  • Close as fast as 7 days or as slow as you need
  • No last-minute renegotiations — our price doesn't change
  • Leave what you don't want — we handle cleanup
  • Cash wired directly to your account at closing
